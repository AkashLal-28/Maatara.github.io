About Us

MAA TARA MICRO CARE FOUNDATION is a private limited company registered & licensed as Non-Profit Making Company (Non-Deposit taking) under section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 and is exempted from the provisions of Chapter III 8 of the RBI Act, 1934.

MAA TARA MICRO CARE FOUNDATION aims at providing financial assistance to the poor from rural and semi-urban families to strengthen their roles in society. A family is an important institution in Indian society. Just like a potter shapes the clay into a desired pot model, a woman is entrusted to shape the future of her family. She is considered as one of the pillars. Company believes in strengthening the role of a woman in her family by providing various means of livelihood and assistance she would need in establishing herself as a micro-entrepreneur. Thus, strengthening her role in the society as a whole..

Our Vision

Our micro care foundation aims to empower women and rural communities by providing accessible and affordable financial services. We focus on promoting financial independence, supporting rural development, and fostering social equality. Through micro loans and financial literacy programs, we aim to help women and rural people start businesses, manage finances, and create sustainable livelihoods. By offering community-centered, environmentally conscious, and inclusive solutions, we envision building a future where women and rural communities thrive economically and socially.
